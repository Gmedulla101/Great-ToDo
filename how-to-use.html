How to use the Great ToDo

The Great ToDo is the easiest and simplest todo app to use oon the web.

To add a new task, simply type it into the input box and click "ADD TASK" to register your task.

To mark a task as done, click the green button.

To edit a task, click the yellow button, and clisk save to finalize your edit and then click cancel to exit the edit mode.

To delete a task that has already been done, click the red button.

Stay productive!!
